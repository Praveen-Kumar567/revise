Batman, also known as Bruce Wayne, is a fictional superhero in the DC Comics universe.  
He is a billionaire philanthropist who fights crime in Gotham City using his intellect and gadgets.  
Unlike many superheroes, Batman has no superpowers, relying on his physical prowess and detective skills.  
His iconic costume includes a bat-shaped cowl, cape, and utility belt.
